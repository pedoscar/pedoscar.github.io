ABOUT US

The Pedoscar are a nonprofit association with the purpose of organizing events in different Italian venues. The Pedoscar were born in 2021, when Jacopo had the idea to create a ceremony in which awards were given to the best memes that the Postaggio group chat had created during the year. At that point, Fabietto proposed making it a live event, in the style of the Oscars. Finally, the first staff group was created: it was composed by Fabietto, Passa, Jacopo, Gianmarco and Trivelli, that gave the event the setting we know today. The Pedoscars are currently in their second year, and have helped to spread joy and fun to the diverse group of Postaggio.
